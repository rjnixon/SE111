About Ryan

In July of 2018, my left leg was crushed in an accident resulting in my being out of work for over 10 months. During that period, due to having more time than I knew what to do with, I bought a ten-dollar course on Web Development from UDEMY. I have always enjoyed computers and technology (in high school I built my own PC); but programming was completely new to me. I spent my remaining months at home seeing what I could do with HTML and CSS until I was finally well enough to return to work. Being back at work was wonderful, but I was always counting down the time each day until I could go home and practice more programming. Nine months after returning to work, I knew there was something I needed to do. Prior to my accident, returning to school was the last thing I was thinking of, but that changed. As a result, my chosen career path at New England Institute of Technology is Software Engineering & Web Development. With the courses I have taken so far, I know that this was the right choice for me, and I feel that NEIT is preparing me in such a way that I will be successful in my endeavor. I have a passion for it that I never thought could happen, and I look forward to starting my new career.
