Review

Ryan Nixon - August 14th, 2020

The Last Samuraiis set in japan in the 1870's a former civil war veteran, Captain Nathan Algren (Tom Cruise) is hired by the emperor of japan to train the countries first army in modern warfare. The imperial soldiers under Captain Nathan Algren were brought into conflict against the samurai due to differing views and were defeated due to their inexperience. Algren was wounded and his life was spared after a courageous stand against the samurai. He found himself impressed with the samurai's way of life and finds himself stuck in the middle of this conflict with only his sense of honor guiding his way.

Trivia

  • Tom Cruise spent almost two years in preparation for this movie, including swordplay instruction, and Japanese-language lessons.
  • This movie made more money at the box office in Japan than in the United States.
  • During filming in New Zealand, Tom Cruise stayed in Oakura. The filming brought over $50 million to the local economy in Taranaki.
